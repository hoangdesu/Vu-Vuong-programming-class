Mission statement

This platform has been designed and developed to address the pressing concerns of household waste management in NSW. Through visualizations and insights based on the data we collect, our platform aims to empower residents, policymakers and ‘nature lovers’ to make informed decisions that benefit the environment. Having a consolidated platform provides users with valuable insights and full data transparency, empowering them to teach and promote— sustainable practices. Furthermore, our website approaches social challenges in a variety of ways. Our useful and accessible content caters to users of all levels, from beginners to advanced learners. Access detailed analysis and statistics, including data on domestic waste and recycling from 2018 to 2022, to find relevant patterns and trends for research purposes.

User manual

Our team built a user-friendly and interactive UI/UX for the website, allowing users to explore with intention and engagement. Our website's design is to educate and inform visitors about this topic. Users can easily access various areas of our website, including statistics/data and solutions/advice, through the navigation bar.

Our website targeted three personas: Rebecca Smith, James Miller, and Arjun Patel. Everyone has a specific interest in Domestic Waste Management and Recycling, and their needs/goals will be covered in greater depth.

Personas

Rebecca Smith

Rebecca is a young professional who is passionate about sustainability and reducing her carbon footprint. She uses our platform to find ways to recycle more effectively in her household.

James Miller

James is a middle-aged father who is concerned about the impact of waste on his local community. He explores statistics on our website to educate his children about the importance of recycling.

Arjun Patel

Arjun is a retired engineer who volunteers in environmental initiatives. He uses our data insights to make presentations about waste management at his community center.
